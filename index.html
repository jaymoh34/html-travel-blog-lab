My Fire Island Adventure

Last summer, I visited Fire Island National Seashore, a beautiful barrier island off the coast of Long Island, New York. The island has miles of pristine beaches, tall sand dunes, and peaceful nature trails.

I spent my days swimming in the ocean, hiking through the forests, and watching amazing sunsets. The highlight was climbing the historic Fire Island Lighthouse and seeing deer wandering through the dunes. It was an unforgettable trip!
